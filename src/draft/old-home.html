What’s an Agreement?

A Nation3 agreement is a contract between two or more parties that is regulated by the laws of  Nation3's legal system. It's akin to a legal contract in a traditional jurisdiction.

The Court

The parties that enter the agreement are legally bound by it, and can be taken to court (ultimately, the Supreme Court) in case they breach the agreement's terms.

Terms, Law & Constitution

The agreement's terms are a document in the English language. The terms can contain any arbitrary legal clauses, but must adhere to the Nation3 Constitution and law in effect.

Collateral assets

In Nation3 agreements, each party enters the agreement with an amount of tokens, which can be taken from them in case of breach to compensate the other parties in the agreement.

Dispute resolutions

In case of misbehavior, any of the parties can open a dispute and the Nation3 courts will resolve the dispute and rule a fair outcome.

Connecting Across the Globe

When two individuals from opposite ends of the globe share a common vision, Nation3 is here to help them connect with ease. Whether it's to work together on a project, share ideas or collaborate on a new business venture, Nation3's platform provides a simple and effective way for these individuals to connect and begin working together.

Creating a Agreement

With Nation3's Legal system, two individuals can create a legally binding agreement that is enforceable and secure, no matter where they are in the world. The Legal system helps to ensure that both parties understand the terms of the agreement and can rely on Nation3's platform to provide security in the process.

Security in the first

Nation3's cloud-based platform is designed to provide maximum security and protection for users throughout the agreement process. The platform allows users to store and manage their agreements in the cloud, ensuring that they are accessible and secure from anywhere in the world. With Nation3, you can be sure that your interests are protected at all times.

Smooth Collaboration

Nation3's platform is designed to provide additional features that can enhance the collaboration between two individuals. The Dispute resolution system allows parties to resolve any issues that may arise during the agreement process in a quick and efficient manner. Additionally, the curated judge system provides a fair and unbiased resolution for any disputes that cannot be resolved through mediation.

Empowering People and DAOs

Nation3's integration with DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations) provides a new way for individuals and organizations worldwide to create agreements in a decentralized manner. With security and transparency provided by Nation3's platform, individuals can trust that their agreements are secure and that their interests are protected. This integration empowers individuals and organizations to make agreements that are more flexible and decentralized than ever before.
